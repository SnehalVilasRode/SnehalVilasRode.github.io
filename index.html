Scalable Database

I am experienced in handling below type of dataset
1) NoSQL databases: NoSQL databases are designed to handle large amounts of data without the need for a traditional relational database structure. They are often used for storing unstructured data, such as social media posts or sensor data.
2) Distributed databases: Distributed databases are spread across multiple servers, which allows them to scale horizontally. This means that they can be easily expanded by adding more servers.
3) Cloud-based databases: Cloud-based databases are hosted on the cloud, which means that they can be accessed from anywhere. This makes them ideal for businesses that need to be able to access their data from multiple locations.

Email Classification

Naive Bayes classifiers are a popular statistical technique of e-mail filtering. They typically use bag of words features to identify spam e-mail, an approach commonly used in text classification. Naive Bayes classifiers work by correlating the use of tokens (typically words, or sometimes other things), with spam and non-spam e-mails and then using Bayes' theorem to calculate a probability that an email is or is not spam.
